    Time and healing
    • Everybody says time heals everything. But I don't think so.
    • I feel, there will be different situations in our life that we go through at different times..
      All those situations make us realise what's really worth our energy, and any emotions that hold us back.
    • So with time, with situations we will know how to deal with and react to them. Though we learn how to live with the pain, somewhere that scar never fades away and keep haunting at us from nowhere.
    • So, this drawing is all about that. Depicting the scars from past, being and enjoying the present in little happiness and a hope and desire about the future.

    Art and Therapy
    • Art is therapy.
    • Brain is the biggest enemy. Sometimes, even for the smallest things we keep thinking alot and get may negative feelings.
    • Dont you think its all becuase of thinking too much. We people don't need to be impressoned in jails oe elsewhere. We are already impressonedin our own thoughts.
    • Sometimes, by expressing our grief through art can make us feel much better instead of waiting for someone to listen to us. and though listened, what if they understand or get you wrong. With art, you have any such extra burdens. You can peacefully express your thoughts and come out of your sadness.
